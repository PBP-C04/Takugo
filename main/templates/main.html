TAKUGO'S STORY

In today's busy world, finding time for traditional book reading can be a challenge. Takugo is here to bridge that gap. We're your one-stop online platform, bringing comic and manga enthusiasts together. Our mission is to create a vibrant community where you can easily purchase, share reviews, and discuss your thoughts on the books you love. Join us and be part of the future of reading!

Indonesia stands out as a significant contributor to the global anime fan community, with two major cities recognized by Google.

Yamane (2020: 73) reveals that 58.5% of respondentsaged 19 to 25 identify as otaku or enjoy anime and manga.

Indonesia ranks 60th out of 61 countries in reading interest according to a study by Central Connecticut State University.

Over 60 million Indonesians have access to gadgets, with more than 100 million active smartphone users in 2018.
